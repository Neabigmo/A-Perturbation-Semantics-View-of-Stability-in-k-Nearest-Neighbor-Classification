\section{Bibliographic Notes}

The bibliography is curated to support three distinct jobs. First, it anchors
the classical algorithmic-stability vocabulary
\cite{bousquet2002stability,kutin2002almost,feldman2019high,bousquet2020sharpening}.
Second, it situates nearest-neighbor performance and deleted-estimate analyses
\cite{cover1967nearest,cover1968estimation,stone1977consistent,rogers1978finite,devroye1979distribution,devroye1979local}.
Third, it records the recent prediction-stability and conformal-certification
papers that motivate keeping LOO-style and replacement-style perturbations
distinct \cite{liang2023algorithmic,pournaderi2024training}.

The computational search infrastructure that provided the initial $1$-NN
witness is described in the supplementary material \cite{codex2025codex}.
